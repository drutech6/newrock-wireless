Newrock Wireless Privacy Notice for California Residents

Effective Date: October 29, 2025 

This Privacy Notice for California Residents supplements the information contained in our general Privacy Policy and applies solely to California consumers, which include our customers, website visitors, job applicants, and employees who are California residents. 

The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), grants California residents specific rights regarding their personal information. This notice outlines those rights and explains how California residents can exercise them. 

1. Information We Collect

Newrock Wireless collects, and has collected within the last 12 months, the following categories of personal information for a business purpose: 

  • Identifiers: Name, address, email address, phone number, account login details, social security number, driverâ€™s license number, and other similar identifiers.

  • Customer Records Information: Information such as bank account number, credit card number, and other financial details necessary for payment.

  • Commercial Information: Records of products or services purchased, obtained, or considered.

  • Internet or Other Electronic Network Activity Information: Browsing and search history on our website or app, and information regarding a consumer's interaction with our network, including IP addresses, data usage, and device model.

  • Geolocation Data: Precise location data from your mobile device.

  • Sensory Information: Voice recordings from customer service calls.

  • Professional or Employment-Related Information: For job applicants and employees.

  • Inferences Drawn from Personal Information: For example, consumer profiles reflecting preferences, characteristics, or behavior. 

2. How We Use Your Personal Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following business purposes: 

  • To provide and maintain the Service.

  • To process and fulfill your orders.

  • To improve our network, products, and services.

  • To personalize your experience.

  • To communicate with you about your account and our services.

  • For security and fraud prevention.

  • For marketing and advertising purposes.

  • To comply with legal obligations. 

3. How We Disclose Your Personal Information

We may disclose your personal information to third parties, including service providers, contractors, and other partners for our business purposes. We have specific contractual agreements with these parties to ensure they handle your data in a manner that is consistent with the CCPA and CPRA. 

4. Your California Privacy Rights

California residents have rights under the CCPA/CPRA, including the right to know what personal information is collected, used, shared, or sold; the right to request deletion or correction of personal information; the right to opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information; and the right to limit the use of sensitive personal information. California residents also have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these privacy rights. 

5. How to Exercise Your Rights

You can submit a verifiable consumer request by visiting the Newrock Wireless Privacy Center website, calling the toll-free number at (855) 948-3686, or by using an authorized agent. We will verify your identity before processing requests. 

6. Responding to Your Requests

Requests will be confirmed within 10 business days and responded to within 45 calendar days. Records of requests will be kept for at least 24 months. 

7. "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" 

California residents can opt-out of the sale or sharing of personal information by clicking the "Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information" link on the website or by enabling the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal in their browser, which we recognize. 

8. Contact Information

For questions or concerns, please contact us via our website or toll-free number.
